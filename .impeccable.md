# Impeccable Design Config — seyfert-web

## Design Context

### Users
Discord bot developers (TypeScript-first, performance-conscious, often running large-scale bots). They arrive from GitHub/Discord/word-of-mouth, usually to evaluate the framework or read the guide. Meme-fluent audience, but they judge a framework's seriousness by its site.

### Brand Personality
Precise, luminous, confident. Seyfert is named after Seyfert galaxies — the brightest active galactic nuclei in the universe — and the brand leans into real astronomy, not sci-fi. Performance claims are backed by real benchmark data. The memetic side of the brand (chad, social credit) exists but is quarantined to the `?chad` easter egg: **zero humor on the default landing**.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **North star references:** Awwwards-tier cinematic 3D (lusion.co, activetheory) + editorial art direction (zajno.com, cosmos.so). The goal is "how was this made?", not "which AI made this?"
- **Palette — real astrophotography (Hubble/JWST):** warm amber-white galactic core, coppery/burnt dust tones, deep blue-black space (never pure #000). Explicitly banned: the AI palette (cyan-on-dark, purple→blue gradients, neon glow accents), gradient text on headings.
- **Hero:** astrophotographic cinematic galaxy — thousands of additive-blended particles, dark dust lanes, breathing core; subtle scroll dive toward the nucleus. WebGL via ogl.
- **Typography:** editorial contrast — a distinctive display face for big moments paired with a refined body face. Monospace only for genuine data annotations (coordinates, metrics), not as "developer vibes" decoration.
- **Layout:** asymmetry and varied rhythm; not everything centered, not everything carded.
- Dark theme only on the landing.

### Design Principles
1. **Astrophotography, not sci-fi** — if a color or effect wouldn't appear in a telescope image, question it.
2. **One extraordinary moment, fully executed** — the galaxy carries the wow; everything else stays quiet and lets it breathe.
3. **Editorial composition** — typography and asymmetric layout do the talking; effects support, never compensate.
4. **Performance is part of the aesthetic** — 60fps, lazy init, pause off-screen, beautiful static fallback under `prefers-reduced-motion`/no-WebGL.
5. **Credibility over decoration** — numbers come from /public/bench data; no decorative sparklines or fake stats.
6. **Personality is quarantined** — `/` is precise and luminous; `?chad` is where the memes live.
